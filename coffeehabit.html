We're sure that's a safe amount of caffeine?

Date Sat 26 March 2016 Tags coffee / opinion

Over time, my coffee habit has grown. I personally think it's a relatively tame amount: somewhere between four and five standard cups per day (so, somewhere between 24 and 30 fluid ounces). It could be worse, I suppose. I started drinking it in the mornings working for groundskeeping at my old university, and since then it's stuck.

My tastes have also changed. I started out with first-wave Maxwell House coffee, and since then I've started grinding my own beans and selecting roasts based on what I know about regional coffee differences and roasting techniques. As time goes on, this blog will also include posts about the different grinding and brewing methods I try. I occasionally take a more empirical approach to my cofee brewing methods, and it's my hope to document those methodological forays.

I'm always open to brewing suggestions! You should feel free to drop me a line (email on the right) if you have good information to share.
